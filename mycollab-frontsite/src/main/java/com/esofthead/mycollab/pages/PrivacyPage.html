Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that is placed on your hard disk by a Web server. Cookies are not used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. MyCollab uses cookies to help MyCollab identify and track your visits and usage of MyCollab website, and your website access preferences. MyCollab users who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using MyCollab’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of MyCollab’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
