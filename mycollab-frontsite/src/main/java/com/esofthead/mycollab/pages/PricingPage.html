How does the 30-day trial work?

When you sign up, you are automatically enrolled in a free 30-day trial that gives you unrestricted access to all the great features Assembla has to offer. During your trial you have the option to cancel at any time. When your trial ends you can choose to remain on your current package, upgrade to one with more users and spaces, downgrade, or cancel.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you may upgrade your plan at any time. Choose a plan that suits your needs today, and upgrade as the numbers of users and spaces grow. After you upgrade the changes will be updated on your next billing cycle.

What if I want to downgrade my plan?

You can downgrade your plan at any time as long as the plan you select is consistent with your current usage. For example, if you currently have 15 spaces, you must delete 5 of your spaces before you can downgrade to a Group plan that allows for up to 10 spaces.

Do I have to provide payment information up front?

No, you can choose the "manual payment" option in the billing information panel. At the end of the trial, we will send you an email to remind you to submit payment information. You can then choose if and how you want to pay.

What payment options are available?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express for automatic payments. We also accept PayPal, checks, and bank wires for manual payments.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No, there are no contracts. You can choose to pay monthly, or you can pay in advance for a year and get 2 months free (12 months for the price of 10). By paying in advance, you can submit one expense report or purchase order for the year.
