How does the 30-day trial work?

When you sign up, you are automatically enrolled in a free 30-day trial that gives you unrestricted access to all the great features MyCollab has to offer. During your free trial, you have the option to cancel at any time. When your trial ends, you can choose to remain on your current package, upgrade to another one with more users and storages, downgrade, or cancel.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you may upgrade your plan at any time. Choose a plan that suits your needs today, and upgrade as the numbers of users and spaces grow. After you upgrade the changes will be updated on your next billing cycle.

What if I want to downgrade my plan?

You can downgrade your package at any time as long as the one you select is consistent with your current usage. For example, if you currently have 30 users, you must delete 10 of them before you can downgrade to a Compact package that allows for up to 20 users.

Do I have to provide payment information up front?

No, you can choose the "manual payment" option in the billing information panel. At the end of the trial, we will send you an email to remind you to submit payment information. You can then choose if and how you want to pay.

What payment options are available?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express for automatic payments. We also accept PayPal, checks, and bank wires for manual payments.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No, there are no contracts. You can choose to pay monthly, or you can pay in advance for a year and get 2 months free (12 months for the price of 10). By paying in advance, you can submit one expense report or purchase order for the year.

How can I install MyCollab on my host?

MyCollab Community Edition is hosted on SourceForge, from which you can download the latest distribution. Before installing MyCollab on your host, make sure that every step in the installation guideline is followed.

What are the differences between the Community Edition and the Professional Edition?

Added cache layer and optimized for high load, the Professional Edition is four times as fast as the Community Edition (the Community Edition is still one of fastest compared to its competitors though). For a more detailed comparison, check out the table below.

Can you change MyCollab for my Organization?

We love making features, customizations, adjustments and tweaks to the system with competitive rate. If fitted, sometimes they may be added to the Standard Version.

Can I get a Discount?

We offer great discounts for annual and bi-annual payments. NPOs, Charities, Startups and Academic Institutions may be entitled to special discounts. If you organization is eligible, please contact an Office Sales Representative of ours.

Can I customize MyCollab by myself?

Yes, MyCollab community is an open source software with license GPL v3. You can do everything with our source code despite having to follow the GPL v3 license if you do not want to be our partner.
